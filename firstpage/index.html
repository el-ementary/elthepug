Frequently asked questions

Is El a dog?

Impossible to say. While she looks and smells like a dog, she has the temperament and mannerisms of a cat. When we put her in a dinosaur outfit (as you do) she also looks like Allie Brosh in that one Hyperbole and a Half comic.


Can I see a photo of El's tail?

Oddly specific, but sure! You'll notice that it's very curly.


What does El like to do in her free time?

El is a creature of a few, very particular habits. She enjoys sleeping: in laps, one of her three beds, or in sunbeams. She appreciates a good walk, although she's a little weird about stepping on grass. But most of all, she really, really, really loves eating. Everything. Everywhere. And (if we don't keep an eye on her), all at once.
